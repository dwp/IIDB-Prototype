What happens next

Your doctor, employer and other medical professionals might be asked to give information about your condition and what caused it. If there's anyone you don't want to be contacted in this way call 0345 758 5433.

You may need a medical assessment - you'll be told when and where this will take place. If you miss an assessment, it could delay your claim.

It can take up to 11 weeks to get a decision depending on the information needed and how quickly people respond.

You don't need to contact anyone before this time if you don't hear anything. Any benefit you're awarded will be backdated.
