If you've had an accident at work or have an illness directly caused by work

You can apply online. To claim you'll need:

  • details of the doctor or clinic you're registered with
  • details of the accident
  • your employment details at the time of the accident
  • National Insurance number
  • your bank details
Form Navigation

If you can't apply online

Print and fill in form BI100A and send it to the address on the form.

If you've developed a disease because of work you did in the past

Print and fill in form BI100PD and send it to the address on the form.

To get a form by post

If you need a form to be sent to you by post, or in a special format then contact the Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit centre.
