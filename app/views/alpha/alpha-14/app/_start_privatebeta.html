Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit

If you've been injured in an accident at work

You can apply online. To claim you'll need:

  • date of the accident
  • details of the doctor or clinic you're registered with
  • details of the accident
  • your employment details at the time of the accident

As part of your application you must give Department for Work and Pensions permission to contact your employer and medical professionals. You can withdraw this permission at any time after you apply.

You'll also usually need a medical examination to assess your condition.
