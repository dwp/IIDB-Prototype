Use this service to claim Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit(IIDB)

The application will take around 10 minutes to complete and to claim you'll need:

  • Details of the accident
  • Details of the medical professional or establishment you visited after you accident (if you visted one)
  • Employment details at the time of the accident
  • National insurance number
  • Bank details
