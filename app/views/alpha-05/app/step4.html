Can I get Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit?

Bank account details

Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit can be paid into a UK bank account or Post Office card account of the person claiming.

You won't be paid Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit without a UK bank account.

You can provide bank account details later by contacting Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit.

Post Office Card Account Numbers

If you're using a post office card account, you're account number isn't the number on your card.

You can find the correct number on any you've received from the post office about your account.
